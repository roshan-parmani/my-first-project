Nukkad Natak for Consumer Rights Day

Empowering Students, Empowering Consumers

Nukkad Natak Script

Scene 1: Introduction

  • A group of students are seen discussing their daily life struggles with consumer products.
  • One of them shares their experience of buying a defective product and not getting a refund.

Scene 2: The Problem

  • The students realize that they are not aware of their consumer rights.
  • They discuss how they are often exploited by companies and sellers.

Scene 3: The Solution

  • A student dressed as a consumer rights activist enters the scene.
  • The activist educates the students about their rights and how to file complaints.

Scene 4: The Conclusion

  • The students are now aware of their rights and feel empowered.
  • They take an oath to spread awareness about consumer rights among their peers.
